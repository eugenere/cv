Super Seal™ Max induction cap sealer
A touch of excellence
The Super Seal™ Max is the latest development in the Super Seal™ Touch range of equipment. This dual coil induction cap sealer offers increased power and a multi-language touch screen for a simpler operation. As one of the fastest in-line sealers on the market, the Super Seal™ Max is designed for high speed applications in the packaging industry. This advanced induction cap sealer delivers reliable sealing with a diverse operating window that compensates for cap, liner and container tolerance variations.

System functionality

  • Multiple language selection
  • Easy to use touch screen design
  • Supervisory password protection with operator lockout
  • Recipe management
  • Internal monitoring of all critical operating data
  • Container, cap and foil counters available

Control & integration options

  • Local and remote start/stop controls
  • Local and remote power controls
  • Remote network control and monitoring via RS485 or Ethernet

Plus

  • Air-cooled
  • Robust stainless steel fully adjustable stand for different container heights
  • Seals a variety of caps ranging from 20mm – 140mm
  • Fully compliant with all CE regulations

Specifications

  • Dimensions 812 x 880 x 1600-1970 mm (WxDxH)
  • Power Supply: single phase, 230V, 50Hz
