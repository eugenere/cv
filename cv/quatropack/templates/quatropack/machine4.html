Super Seal™ 50 Induction Cap Sealer
The Best Selling Induction Cap Sealer


These best-selling models seal millions of bottles worldwide every day and are renowned for their reliability and robustness.

Compact, all-in-one unit

Compact and air-cooled, the Super Seal™ is easily installed into existing production lines, while the simple to use control panel ensures ease of operation. The stainless-steel stand is fully adjustable to allow for different container heights and is also suitable for food or clean environments.

Specifications

  • Dimensions 580 x 880 x 1524-1824mm (WxDxH)
  • Power Supply: single phase, 230V, 50Hz, 5amps

Features

  • Air-cooled
  • Efficient coil designs to maximise performance and minimise energy costs
  • Simple to use control panel
  • Fully adjustable stand for different container heights
  • Seals a variety of caps ranging from 20mm – 140mm
  • Fully compliant with all CE regulations
  • Power supply electronics are housed in an IP65 enclosure
